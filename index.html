Let's Troubleshoot Together

Below are a few common solutions. If you still need help, scroll down to Contact & Support to chat with us directly.

Qiblah Compass or Prayer Times Not Updating

Make sure your device’s location services are enabled and permissions are granted.
• Android: Settings > Apps > Tawqeer Al-Quran > Permissions > Location
• iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services

After enabling location, try refreshing from the “Prayer Time” page or relaunch the app.

Azan Notifications Not Appearing

Grant Notification Permissions:
go to Settings > Notifications > Tawqeer Al-Quran
• iOS: Check Settings > Notifications and ensure alerts are allowed.

Disable Battery Optimization: (Especially on Android)
• Some devices halt background processes to save battery.
• Go to Settings > Battery > Tawqeer Al-Quran > Unrestricted to keep Azan notifications running.

Audio Recitations Not Working

• Check that your internet connection is stable, as streaming may require decent bandwidth.
• Make sure media volume is not muted.
• Ensure “Play Azan Audio” is turned on in the app’s Settings.

Language or Font Issues

• If text is unreadable or misaligned, go to Settings > Language or Settings > Font Size within the app’s main menu.
• Ensuring your chosen language and size load properly.

The more details you include, the faster we can help you resolve the issue.
