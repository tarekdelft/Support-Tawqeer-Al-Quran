Tawqeer Al-Quran – Support

Welcome to Tawqeer Al-Quran

Tawqeer Al-Quran is a multifaceted app designed to enrich your spiritual routine. This page provides troubleshooting tips and contact information for any issues you may experience. Please see below for setup guidance and frequently asked questions.

Key Features

  • Qiblah Compass: Accurately determines the direction of the Kaaba to help you pray in the correct direction.
  • Prayer Times & Azan Notifications: Stay on schedule with up-to-date prayer times and optional Azan alerts.
  • Location-Based Services: Customize your location using permissions to get precise Qiblah directions and prayer schedules.
  • Audio Recitations: Listen to soulful Quran recitations integrated with audioplayers and just_audio_background.
  • Language Support: Toggle seamlessly between Arabic, English, and Bangla, adjusting font size as you prefer.
  • Full Quran Access (Optional Reading Plans): For those who wish, the app offers easy navigation by Surah, Juz, or page.

Troubleshooting

1. Qiblah Compass or Prayer Times Not Updating

Make sure your device’s location services are enabled and permissions are granted. On Android, go to Settings > Apps > Tawqeer Al-Quran > Permissions to enable Location. On iOS, check Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services.

2. Azan Notifications Not Appearing

Grant Notification Permissions: - Android 13+: Use Permission.notification.request() in the app or Settings > Notifications > Tawqeer Al-Quran. - iOS: Check Settings > Notifications and ensure alerts are allowed.

Disable Battery Optimization: (Especially on Android) - Some devices halt background processes to save battery. - Go to Settings > Battery > Tawqeer Al-Quran > Unrestricted to keep Azan notifications running.

3. Audio Recitations Not Working

Check that your internet connection is stable, as streaming may require decent bandwidth. Also ensure media volume is not muted and the app’s “Play Azan Audio” option is turned on.

4. Language or Font Issues

If text is unreadable or misaligned, go to Settings > Language or Settings > Font Size within the app’s main menu. This is managed through _getGlobalFontFamily() in main.dart, ensuring your chosen language and size load properly.
