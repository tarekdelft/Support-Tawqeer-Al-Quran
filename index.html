Let's Troubleshoot Together

Below are a few common solutions. If you still need help, scroll down to Contact & Support to chat with us directly.

My Qiblah Compass or Prayer Times won’t update

Ensure Location Services are turned on:
• Android: Settings > Apps > Tawqeer Al-Quran > Permissions > Location
• iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services

After enabling location, try refreshing from the “Prayer Time” page or relaunch the app.

My phone doesn’t ring the Azan

• Check Notification Permissions: Android 13+ requires you to explicitly allow notifications for each app. iOS users can enable alerts in Settings > Notifications.
• Disable Battery Optimization (Android). Some devices stop background processes to save battery. Mark Tawqeer Al-Quran as Unrestricted or Not Optimized.

Can’t hear audio recitations

• Confirm “Play Azan Audio” is toggled on in Settings.
• Check that your volume level is not muted or on Silent.
• Verify you have a stable internet connection if streaming is needed.

Language or font looks off

• Go to Settings > Language or Font Size inside the app. We provide Arabic, English, and Bangla, plus adjustable font sizes.
• The app’s _getGlobalFontFamily() in main.dart ensures each language loads properly.

Need More Help?

Our support team is here for deeper questions or unusual bugs—like repeated app crashes or missing features. When emailing us, kindly share:

  • Your Device Model (e.g., Samsung Galaxy S21, iPhone 13 Pro)
  • Your OS Version (e.g., Android 13 or iOS 16)
  • Which App Version you’re on (Find in your phone's App Info, or in the “About” section in-app.)
  • A quick description of What Went Wrong and how to reproduce it.

The more details you include, the faster we can help you resolve the issue.
